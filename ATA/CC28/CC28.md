## Notes on Extension Articles A6–A9

Articles A0–A5 cover the **classical ATA 28 structure** and carry full normative weight from ATA iSpec 2200 and CS-25. They are backward-compatible with all existing AMPEL360 Q100 documentation.

Articles A6–A9 are **reserved for scientific and future-technology extensions**:

- **A6** — Hydrogen propulsion (directly linked to AMPEL360 Q100's LH₂ PEM fuel cell architecture)
- **A7** — Thermal management (fuel-as-coolant, critical for electric/hybrid propulsion)
- **A8** — Autonomous logistics (forward-looking; drone and UAM applicability)
- **A9** — Digital twin (spans all A0–A8 articles; provides system-level observability and prediction)

This design ensures the framework is **forward-compatible** without breaking historical ATA numbering or requiring re-certification of classical articles when new technology articles are added.
