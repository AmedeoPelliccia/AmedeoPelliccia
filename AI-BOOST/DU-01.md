# DU-01 — Military Application of Simulation Acceleration

> **Programme:** AI-BOOST — GA 101135737 (EuroHPC JU)
> **Parent deliverable:** [DEL-07 Ethics and Security](DEL-07-ethics-and-security.md)
> **Machine-readable source:** [`ethics-and-security.yaml`](ethics-and-security.yaml) § `dual_use.risk_analysis[0]`
> **Related:** [Dual-Use Risk Analysis](dual-use-risk-analysis.md)

---

## 1. Risk Description

AI surrogate models could accelerate weapons system design.

## 2. Risk Assessment

| Dimension | Rating |
|-----------|--------|
| Likelihood | Low |
| Impact | **High** |
| Residual risk (post-mitigation) | Low |

## 3. Mitigation

- Export-control screening of consortium partners.
- Restricted model variants for defence-adjacent use cases.
- All consortium partners screened against EU consolidated sanctions lists and dual-use entity lists.
- Model distribution carries responsible use license terms prohibiting military end-use without prior authorisation.

## 4. Export Control

| Requirement | Status | Evidence |
|-------------|:------:|----------|
| EU Dual-Use Regulation (EU) 2021/821 screening | Planned | All consortium partners screened; no listed entities |
| US EAR/ITAR assessment | Planned | No US-origin controlled technology used in core pipeline |
| End-use monitoring | Planned | Responsible use license terms for model distribution |

## 5. Review Schedule

Re-assessed at each consortium review (M6, M18, M36).
