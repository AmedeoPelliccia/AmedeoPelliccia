# DU-03 — Surveillance Capability

> **Programme:** AI-BOOST — GA 101135737 (EuroHPC JU)
> **Parent deliverable:** [DEL-07 Ethics and Security](DEL-07-ethics-and-security.md)

## 1. Risk Description

Multilingual NLP could be repurposed for mass surveillance.

## 2. Risk Assessment

| Dimension | Rating |
|-----------|--------|
| Likelihood | Low |
| Impact | **High** |
| Residual risk (post-mitigation) | Low |

## 3. Mitigation

- No biometric processing capability is included in the model architecture.
- Data processing agreements explicitly prohibit surveillance use.
- EU AI Act classification confirms no prohibited AI practices (no social scoring, no real-time biometric identification, no manipulation techniques).

## 4. Verification

- Architecture review confirms absence of biometric processing pipelines.
- Contractual safeguards verified in consortium agreements.
- Periodic review at milestones M6, M18, and M36.
